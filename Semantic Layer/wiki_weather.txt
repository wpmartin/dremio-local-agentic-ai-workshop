**Dataset of weather in New York City.**

The raw, sample iceberg table has been formatted to update data types and column names.

**Column name changes:**
* "name" to "location_name"
* "date" to "calendar_date",
* "awnd" to "average_wind",
* "prcp" to "precipitation",
* "snwd" to "snow_depth",
* "tempmax" to "temp_max",
* "tempmin" to "temp_min".

**Data type changes:**
* "date" converted to 'YYYY-MM-DD' date format,
* average_wind converted to float,
* precipitation converted to float,
* snow converted to float,
* snow_depth converted to float,
* temp_max converted to float,
* temp_min converted to float.
